The wood of logs of poplar trees are now sold in 2 categories.

1 Over/Under- size above 14 inch

2 Sokta - size between 14 to 10 inch.

3 firewood - size below 10 inch.

The rates prevalling at Yamunanagar mandi are Over/Under -Rs 950 - Rs 850.

sokta - Rs 560 per quintal.

Firewood - Rs 250 per quintal.
